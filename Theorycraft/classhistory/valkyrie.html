Valkyrie

Class Changes 15 - TBR

Full Moon

  • Normal effect
    • For the first trigger:effect has changed where it increases the damage per tick by 10% instead of 20%. It's still up to 5 stacks. Gives buff to stop lunar gauge from decreasing for 60 seconds.
    • For the second trigger: effect changed where this does more damage the higher stacks you've accumulated.
    • Removal of party buff.
  • Effect with Blood Moon active
    • For the first trigger: effect has changed where it increases the damage per tick by 10% instead of 20%. It's still up to 5 stacks. Gives buff to stop lunar gauge from decreasing for 60 seconds. You also earn +1 moonlight point per stack.
    • For the second trigger: effect changed where this does more damage the higher stacks you've accumulated. You also earn +2 moonlight point per stack.
    • Removal of party buff.
  • Resonance Strike Decrease base damage by 10%.
  • Moon Burst Decrease base damage by 5%.
