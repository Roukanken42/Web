    • Class Changes 7

  • Damage increase on normal monsters and BAMs but not dungeon bosses of the following skills: Divine Radiance, Shocking Implosion, and Final Reprisal.

    • Class Changes 8

  • Skill Damage Upgrade Significantly increased damage against boss monsters.
  • Divine Radiance The projectile speed increased by 20%. Reduce the animation lock towards the end of this skill.
  • Final Reprisal Skill range increased from 4m to 5m. Can be used as a chain skill after Zenobia’s Vortex. A new XI version will be added so any level 65 players need to manually learn this skill to upgrade. This skill will unlock at level 6 instead of 10.
  • Shocking Implosion Cooldown decreased from 15 to 10 seconds.
  • Triple Nemesis Using this skill consecutively is 30% faster. You are able to chain into Final Reprisal after the 1st or 2nd consecutive attack.
  • Focus Heal Improved lock-on detection for faster execution.
  • Divine Intervention A player being pulled by this skill will be able to act much quicker than before.
  • Kaia’s Shield Changed where it takes priority to apply towards the caster of the skill first before reaching to other party members.
  • Blessing of Shakan Combines Blessing of Shakan and Blessing of Zenobia altogether. All the glyphs from these combined skills will be added to this new skill. These individual buff skills will be removed in the process.
  • Blessing of Balder Combines Blessing of Seren, Blessing of Balder, and Blessing of Arachne altogether. All the glyphs from these combined skills will be added to this new skill. These individual buff skills will be removed in the process.

    • Class Changes 9

  • Backstep Cooldown reduced by 1 second. I-frame duration has been slightly increased. New Glyph of Energy added to reduce cooldown of this skill by 10%. Glyph costs 2 points.

    • Class Changes 10

  • Blessing of Shakan Buff only applies to party members. Priest must be present in the party to keep this buff. Range has increased from 6m to 9m. From Priest's perspective it's 9m in front in a 12m diameter.
  • Blessing of Balder Buff only applies to party members. Priest must be present in the party to keep this buff. MP cost reduced from 1,775 to 875. Range has been increased to match with Blessing of Shakan.
  • Kaia's Shield Only applies to party members. Priest must be present in the party to keep this buff.
  • Arise Only applies to party members.
  • Mana Charge MP recovery has been increased on each charge from 4%/10%/20% to 8%/15%/32% from their max MP. Now works up to 25 players.
  • Triple Nemesis Glyph of Enfeeblement (lower target's endurance) will no longer stack with Mystic's Glyph of Enfeeblement from Volley of Curses. Glyph of Enfeeblement will overwrite Mystic's endurance debuff effect.
  • Energy Stars Buff only applies to party members. This buff cannot be avoided anymore when accidentally using an i-frame.
  • Resurrect Glyph of Heartening changed to give max HP & MP.
  • Homeward Bound Removed.

    • Class Changes 11

  • Blessing of Shakan Buff will work up to 30 players in your group. Buff will disappear after teleporting out of the dungeon. Using a teleportal to move within the same dungeon will still persist.
  • Blessing of Balder Buff will work up to 30 players in your group. Buff will disappear after teleporting out of the dungeon. Using a teleportal to move within the same dungeon will still persist.
  • Mana Charge Effected up to 25 players now changed to 30 players.
  • Healing Circle The skill will take priority to heal whoever has the lowest HP.
  • Kaia's Shield The effect will take priority to shield whoever has the lowest HP. Buff will disappear after teleporting out of the dungeon. Using a teleportal to move within the same dungeon will still persist.
