    • Class Changes 3

  • Instance Matching System: Can now be queued as a tank starting at level 65 after completing the quest.

  • Fiery Rage: Version VI learned at level 65 where using this skill in Intimidation stance will draw aggro 10m where you stand, but the buff effects are negated. Cooldown will be reduced to 10 seconds and MP cost becomes 300. All of this happens if you are in Intimidation stance.
  • Unchained Anger: Version VIII learned at level 65 where you sacrifice 300 MP to recover 9,724 HP. Cooldown become 60 seconds. All of this happens if you are in Backdraft stance.
  • Raze: Version I learned at level 61 where skill increases threat generation. You can activate this skill faster if you first use Punishing Strike or Axe Counter first.
  • Staggering Strike: Version V will have increased stun duration when in Intimidation stance. Will not spin target around.
  • Releasing 5 new skills starting at level 65 where you will receive a quest at Highwatch to complete in order to acquire these skills.
