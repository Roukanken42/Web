Archer Revamp

Velik's Mark:

  • The skill will now work for all Archers in the party.
  • Increased damage by 20% will be lowered to 10%.

Dash:

  • A new skill added that allows you to dash up to 16 meters or until you hit a collusion. If you use this while pressing a directional button, you will dash in that direction.
  • Skill learn at level 32.
  • MP costs 188.
  • 25 seconds cooldown.

Find Weakness:

  • A new skill added that gives a buff for 8 seconds where the next attack will count as hitting monsters from behind regardless of your positioning.
  • Skill learn at level 40.
  • MP costs 200.
  • 15 seconds cooldown.

Rain of Arrows:

  • The first hit that lands on your target will always hit in that specific spot for the remaining hits regardless if the target or yourself are turning.
  • Base damage increased by 10%. II version increased from 8956 to 9852.
  • New visuals.

Penetrating Arrow:

  • Base damage increased by 25%. XII version increased from 3849 to 4809.

Radiant Arrow:

  • Base damage increased by 25%. IX version increased from 5885 to 7357.
  • Glyph of Carving (rare) changed from double to triple crit chance. Glyph point reduced from 4 to 3.

Rapid Fire:

  • Gain 1 stack of focus for each successful hit.
  • Total base damage increased by 37%.
    • BEFORE: Rapid Fire IX Base DMG - 406, 269, 269, 269, 269, 269, 1110.
    • AFTER: Rapid Fire IX Base DMG - 1219, 296, 296, 296, 296, 296, 1221.
  • Glyph of Persistence changed from 10% to 15% chance to reset cooldown.
  • Glyph of Power lowered from 50% to 20%.

Arrow Focus:

  • Arrow will continue to receive 1 Arrow Focus per hit. Rapid Fire and Final Shot added to gain Arrow Focus.
  • Upon earning 10 stacks of Arrow Focus, you'll earn a buff for the entire 10 seconds to increase charging speed of any charging skills.

Thunderbolt:

  • Base damage increased by 100%. Increased from 5355 to 10711.
  • 2 Noctenium Infusions will be consumed.
  • Noctenium damage bonus increased to 9~13%.
  • New visuals added.
  • Glyph of Carving added to increase crit chance by two. Uncommon master glyph - 4 points.
  • Glyph of Carving added to increase crit chance by three. Rare master glyph - 3 points.
  • Increased damage against monsters with lower HP has been removed to make it deal damage based on the tooltip's base damage now.
  • PvP damage is lowered by 30%.

Explosion Trap:

  • Base damage increased by 20%. II version increased from 3190 to 3828.

Sniper's Eye:

  • Effect changed from "Increased damage to boss monsters by 5%" to "Increased damage to all monsters by 7%".

Eagle's Eye:

  • Stance is removed.

Poison Arrow:

  • Casting time has been reduced.
  • Poison stacks can go up to three times.

Sequential Fire:
