Class Changes 6

Stats

  • Damage done to boss monsters only are reduced by 7.5%.
  • Overall PvP damage has been lowered by 20%.

Deadly Fury:

  • Cooldown will now be displayed on the skill's icon.

Jackhammer:

  • A fully stacked endurance debuff will decrease from 12% to 8%.
  • Endurance debuff will be overwritten by Warrior's Combative Strike and Berserker's Punishing Strike.

Rampage:

  • This skill will no longer knock-up players in PvP.

Counter:

  • This skill that are chained from Punch cannot block attacks in PvP but using Counter on its own will still block just fine.

Defensive skills:

  • Executing a perfect defense on skills will now award you 40% instead of 50% increased skill damage.
